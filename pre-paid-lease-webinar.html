About the webinar

Pre-paid leases are suddenly at the center of residential solar conversations in 2026. With the end of 25D and homeowner economics shifting, many installers are asking the same question: When do pre-paid leases win over loans, and what does that mean for close rates, cash flow, and operations?

In this special webinar, we're teaming up with heavyweights in the residential solar industry to share:

  • When pre-paid leases beat loans and when they don't
  • What changes operationally after the sale
  • How to position it with homeowners

The event will also include a Q&A where solar installers can ask their burning questions and get tactical advice they can use immediately.
